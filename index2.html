Huitzilopochtli

The Myth from the Coyolxauhqui stone

Huitzilopochtli pursued them, he chased them like rabbits,

all around the mountain ...

with nothing could they defend themselves.

Huitzilopochtli chased them,

he drove them away,

he humbled them,

he destroyed them,

he annihilated them


Carrasco, David. “The Religion of the Aztecs: Ways of the Warrior, Words of the Sage”. Religions of Mesoamerica. Waveland Press, INC
