Quetzalcoatl

Anales de Cuauhtitlan

And they said of the first men,

their god made them, fashioned them of ashes.

their god made them, fashioned them of ashes.

This they attributed to the god Quetzalcoatl,

whose gin is 7-Wind,

he made them, he invented them.

The first Sun or age which was founded,

its sign was 4-Water,

it was called the Sun of Water.

Then it happend

that water carried away eveything.

The people were changed into fish.


Leon-Portilla, Miguel. “Myths in Pre-Columbian Poetry”. Pre-Columbian Literature of Mexico. University Of Oklahoma Press
